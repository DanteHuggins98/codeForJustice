About US

Cardinal Group is a non-profit organization that stands against all forms of human trafficking.
The group was founded in 2022 to bring awareness to the different types of human trafficking, while also informing the public what they can do to help prevent human trafficking

Our Mission

Bringing awareness to modern day slavery in the many forms of human trafficking, while providing resources to rehabilitate victims. We fight human trafficking through prevention, intervention, and aftercare work.

Our Values

Integrity
Accountability
Passion
Humility
Unity
Agility

Our Vision

To strive for a world that is free from all forms of human-trafficking, whether it's sex trafficking, labor trafficking, or domestic servitude.
